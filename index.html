Introduction

When you visit Japan either for business or sightseeing, you might need time to relax and take a rest. You probably would like to stay your hotel or go to hot spring place, but there is adventurous and reasonable option for you... Sento, Japanese public bath.

You will have a very unique experiences in Sento.
